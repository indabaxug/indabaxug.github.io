General Application Guidelines

APPLICATION GUIDELINES

We welcome both students, academicians, researchers and industry professionals to apply. As much as we want everyone to join us, there is limited space available so we shall review applications and contact successful applicants.

Successful applicants will be contacted with instructions on how to register for the conference and confirm attendance.

Applications Close : 1st July, 2021 23:00

Application acceptance notification : ~ By 11th July, 2021 23:55

Event Timing : 26th - 30th July, 2021

Venue : Silver Springs Hotel - Bugolobi, Kampala, Uganda

For this year’s IndabaXUG, We shall focus on the social impact of AI. Students, AI researchers, academicians and Industry experts are highly encouraged to apply.

SPOTLIGHT TALKS, POSTER PRESENTATIONS AND AI APPLICATIONS DEMO (EXHIBITION)

The workshop will feature invited talks from prominent researchers and practitioners, oral presentations, spotlight talks, poster session and an Exhibition. There will also be socials to facilitate networking, discussion of different career opportunities in AI, and sharing of ideas to increase participation of Ugandan researchers in the field. We invite all members of the AI community to apply and attend the workshop.

We welcome research papers, position papers, survey papers, vision papers, or papers that inform about neglected/abandoned areas of study where AI could be impactful. Papers may introduce new theory, methodology, applications or product demonstrations.
